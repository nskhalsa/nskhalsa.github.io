One topic I find interesting is the topic of Star Wars. It's interesting how a lot of people seem to have a different favorite Star Wars movie and it seems that age is a factor in causing a difference. People who were children when the Original Trilogy came out tend to choose one of those three films as their favorite Star Wars film while people who were children when the Prequel Trilogy came out tend to choose one of those three films as their favorite.
